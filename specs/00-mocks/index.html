Hierarchy-Native KPI Tracker

Static UI mocks for the Jira Forge app. Build a KPI tree, record values over time, associate KPIs with work items, and inherit targets down the native issue hierarchy.

i Non-functional mocks styled to look like Jira Cloud. Two hierarchies are in play: the KPI Tree (default top-level KPIs — Revenue, Costs, Opportunity Enablement — broken into their driving levers) and the Jira issue hierarchy (Outcome → Initiative → Increment → Epic → Story).
