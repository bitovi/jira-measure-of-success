KPI Tracker/ Coverage

Coverage design exploration

How a parent's KPI target is covered by the targets its children commit to. The same catalog KPI (say Revenue) can sit on many issues — each issue's target is that issue's slice, distinguished by its host issue (Revenue · INIT‑48), not by a separate catalog entry.

i Additive vs. independent. A field on the KPI definition decides how slices combine. Sum KPIs (Revenue, # of Stores, Orders) add their children's slices toward the parent's target — coverage = Σ(children) vs. parent. Independent KPIs (NPS, Error Rate, Cycle Time) are never summed; each issue's target stands on its own and is compared, not added.
